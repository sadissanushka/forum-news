Our Story

How DiscussDaily Began

Founded in 2020, DiscussDaily started with a simple mission: create a platform where people can engage in meaningful, respectful discussions about the topics that matter most.

Our founder, Alex Chen, recognized that social media was becoming increasingly polarized and that nuanced conversations were becoming harder to find. Drawing from experience in journalism and community building, Alex set out to build a different kind of discussion platform.

Our Growth

What began as a small forum with just a few hundred users has now grown into a thriving community of over 1 million members. Throughout our growth, we've maintained our commitment to fostering thoughtful dialogue and providing high-quality, factual information.

Our Values

  • Intellectual Curiosity: We believe in asking questions and challenging assumptions.
  • Respect: We value diverse perspectives and treat all members with dignity.
  • Evidence: We prioritize facts and reasoned arguments over opinions.
  • Inclusion: We work to make our platform accessible and welcoming to all.
